Personal projects

MapChart

Web app for creating your own custom map of World, Europe, US, and more with colors and descriptions of your choice.

Developed in 2014, it has since been used by millions of people all over the world. Regularly visited by a diverse audience including students, university teachers, sales executives, business owners, research staff, travellers, and more.

Currently the #1 Google search result in its category and the most complete map-making tool overall.

HTML, CSS, JS & jQuery, PHP, Node.js, Nunjucks, SVG
Historical MapChart

The historical version of MapChart featuring maps from different chronologies in history.

A favourite of history buffs, it includes World War maps, historical USA maps and more.

HTML, CSS, JS & jQuery, PHP, Node.js, Nunjucks, SVG
MapChart Mobile App

Android and iOS app bringing all features of MapChart in a native mobile app.

Developed in 2020, after numerous requests from users of the website, it is now the only map-making mobile app available. Boasting over 5,000 downloads and a high review score (over 4,5 stars) on both the Play Store and the App Store.

Flutter, Dart, iOS, Firebase, Android, Kotlin, Swift, ASO
CreateFormation

Web app for making football formations with photos of players.

The only formation creator with photos of all players. A database containing 18,000+ current players and 100+ legends.

HTML, CSS, JS & jQuery, Express.js, AWS, Heroku, MongoDB
